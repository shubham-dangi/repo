Donate, Save, Smile

We can all get by with a little help from our friends.

GiveIndia is a donation platform that allows you to support a cause of your choice from about 200 NGOs that have been scrutinised for transparency and credibility. Established in December 1999, GiveIndia ensures that your contribution reaches the organisation you are passionate about and keeps you updated about its progress.

We make a living by what we get. We make a life by what we give.

It's not how much we give but how much love we put into giving.
